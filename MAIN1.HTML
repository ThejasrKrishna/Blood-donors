Blood, fluid pumped by the heart that circulates throughout the body via the arteries, veins, and capillaries . An adult male of average size normally has about 5.6 liters of blood. The blood carries oxygen and nutrients to the body tissues and removes carbon dioxide and other wastes. The colorless fluid of the blood, or plasma, carries the red and white blood cells, platelets, waste products, and various other cells and substances.
